about

Born in London and raised on the island of Mallorca, Spain.

My passion for filmmaking comes from back in the day, shooting skate videos.

The moment I fell in love with fiction was on set during the shoot of my first short film ("Behind the Mirror").

I remember the feeling, I stopped and looked around me. It blew me away to think that a few days ago I had imagined this place, these characters, these props this lighting…

It was like living inside of my imagination. Everything was in place.

“I gained my experience working as an assistant director around Europe and Australia”
